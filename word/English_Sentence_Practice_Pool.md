# English Sentence Practice Pool

Purpose: Store long or high-value English sentences extracted from user-provided articles for daily English-to-Chinese practice.

Selection factors:
- Professional terminology
- Advanced expression
- Fixed phrase or collocation
- Long or syntactically complex sentence

Difficulty rule:
- Level 1: one factor
- Level 2: two factors
- Level 3: three factors
- Level 4: four factors
- Level 5: exceptionally dense, abstract, or multi-clause sentence

Daily sampling priority:
- Higher difficulty first
- Lower practice count first
- Recently under-practiced sentences first
- Lower recent score first
- Sentences with repeated terminology or structure errors first

Record format:

```text
ID:
Article:
Source:
Sentence:
Factors:
Difficulty:
Practice count:
Last practiced:
Notes:
```

Scoring fields to add when available:

```text
Last score:
Average score:
Error tags:
```

Scoring rule:
- 90-100: accurate terminology, correct structure, natural Chinese.
- 75-89: main meaning correct, with minor terminology or naturalness issues.
- 60-74: core meaning partly correct, but key phrase, clause relation, or logic needs repair.
- Below 60: major misunderstanding of main structure, terminology, or logic.

Review priority rule:
- Any sentence below 75 should be prioritized in the next few sessions.
- Any sentence below 60 should be repeated until it reaches at least 80 once.
- Error tags should guide future selection, such as terminology, main clause, modifier, logic, or Chinese naturalness.
